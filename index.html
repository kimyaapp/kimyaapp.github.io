KIMYA is a smart utility that automatically manages your phone’s Focus modes based on your location, time, and real-world context.

Set it once — it just works.

Your Phone, Smarter.

Spatial Automation

Create geofenced zones like "Home" or "Work" to automatically trigger Focus modes when you enter or leave.

Temporal Automation

Schedule Focus modes based on time of day or specific events, ensuring you’re never disturbed during important moments.

Physical Automation

KIMYA detects if your phone is face-down, in your pocket, or if you're driving, activating the right Focus mode instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

KIMYA is designed exclusively for iOS to leverage its deep integration with Focus modes, Core Location, and other native frameworks. There are no plans for an Android version at this time.

Your privacy is paramount. All location and contextual data is processed directly on your device and is never sent to any external servers. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

KIMYA requires an iPhone running iOS 16 or later to take full advantage of the latest Focus mode and background processing capabilities.
