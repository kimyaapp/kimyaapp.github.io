Help & FAQ

Zones are geographical areas you define where KIMYA automatically manages your Focus modes. When you enter or exit a zone, the app triggers the automation rules you've configured.

KIMYA uses your device's motion sensors to detect when you're in a moving vehicle. When driving is detected, it can automatically enable Do Not Disturb to keep you safe on the road.

Contact exceptions allow specific people to reach you even when Focus mode is active. You can set different exception rules for each zone or automation type.

KIMYA is optimized for minimal battery impact. It uses iOS's efficient location services and only activates when entering or exiting zones.

Yes! All location processing happens locally on your device. KIMYA never sends your location data to any servers. Zone data syncs via your private iCloud account if enabled.

KIMYA is paid-only. Choose Monthly or Yearly—both include full access to all features: context-aware automation (spatial, temporal, physical), rules, calendar integration, contact exceptions, and native iOS UI.

Due to iOS restrictions, third-party apps cannot directly control Focus modes. KIMYA detects your context and sends smart suggestions to help you enable the right Focus mode at the right time.

Make sure you've granted 'Always Allow' location permission and enabled Background App Refresh. Also check that you haven't exceeded the 20-zone iOS limit for geofencing.
