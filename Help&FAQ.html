Hilfe & FAQ

KIMYA automatically manages your Focus (Do Not Disturb) mode based on location (zones), time, and physical context (driving, pocket, face-down).

Location is required to detect when you enter or leave zones. Motion helps identify driving or face-down states. Without these permissions, automation cannot function.

Zones are saved places like Home or Work. Entering/leaving a zone can trigger automation such as Silent Mode, Focus Mode, or Custom Actions.

Yes. Use Exceptions to allow VIP contacts (calls/messages) even when Focus is active.

iOS limits geofencing to 20 zones. KIMYA allows permanent Home & Work plus up to 6–8 pinned zones.

Check that:
• Location is set to Always Allow in iOS Settings.
• Background App Refresh is enabled.
• Motion & Fitness is enabled.
