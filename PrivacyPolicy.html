Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 22, 2025

At KIMYA, your privacy is our top priority. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Information We Collect

  • Location data for zone-based automation
  • Motion and activity data for context detection
  • Device settings and Focus mode preferences
  • Usage analytics to improve the app

How We Use Your Data

  • Enable automatic Focus mode management
  • Detect driving and other activities
  • Provide personalized zone suggestions
  • Improve app performance and features

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is processed locally on your device
  • Zone data is synced via iCloud (if enabled)
  • We use industry-standard encryption
  • No personal data is shared with third parties

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data at any time
  • Delete your data by removing the app
  • Opt-out of analytics in Settings
  • Control all permissions in iOS Settings

Third-Party Services

  • Apple Maps for location services
  • iCloud for data synchronization
  • StoreKit for subscription management
  • No advertising networks or trackers

For any questions or concerns regarding your privacy, please contact us at contact@kimyaapp.com.
