Privacy Policy

KIMYA is designed with privacy in mind:

  • We do not collect or store personal data outside your device.
  • Location, motion, and calendar data are only used locally to automate Focus modes.
  • Subscription and payment processing are handled entirely by Apple via the App Store.
  • No user accounts, external servers, or third-party analytics are integrated.

By using KIMYA, you agree to this privacy policy. If you have concerns, you may contact us at contact@kimyaapp.com.
